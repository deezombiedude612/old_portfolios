LEGEND

WD - - Withdrawn Withdrawn from a module before census date, typically mid semester.
IN - - Incomplete An interim notation given for a module where a student has not completed certain requirements with a valid reason or it is not possible to finalise the grade by the published deadline.
P - - Pass Given for satisfactory completion of a non-graded module.

Note: Non-graded module is module that is 'graded' with 'pass' or 'fail' without any grades such as Grade A, B, C or D.
AU - - Audit Given for a module where attendance is for information only without earning academic credit.
* - - - Module repeated; Previous grade(s) indicated with "*" is/are not calculated in CGPA.
** - - - Module granted supplementary assessment.
