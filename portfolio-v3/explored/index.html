Explored Series

A series of classes making up 3 modules in the explored series. This webpage contains all my scribbles and thoughts as according to the Follow Up questions for each chapter.

These scribbles are based on the sessions I attended in Klang Chinese Methodist Church, Malaysia. Do take note that there may not be any scribble links available if there is no Follow Up section in a certain session attended.

What's the best news you've ever heard?

April 2019 - June 2019

Synopsis (from Christianity Explored Ministries website):
Was it surprising? Did it change the way you think? Or was it something you wanted to be true but didn't dare hope?
Christianity Explored is a relaxed and informal way of sharing the best news you can ever hear, and gives people space and time to think about the big questions of life.
Over seven sessions in Mark’s Gospel, guests find out more about the life of the person at the heart of the Christian faith – Jesus Christ.

Session 1: Good News (April 28, 2019) Session 2: Identity (May 5, 2019) Session 3: Sin (May 12, 2019) Session 4: The Cross (May 19, 2019) Session 5: Resurrection (May 26, 2019) Session 6: Grace (June 2, 2019) Day Away 1: The sower (June 9, 2019) Day Away 2: James and John (June 16, 2019) Day Away 3: Herod (June 16, 2019) Session 7: Come and Die (June 16, 2019)

What's the best gift God could give you?

July 2019 - August 2019

Synopsis (from Christianity Explored Ministries website):
We all want to be happy. Why is lasting happiness so hard to find?
Life Explored is an exciting new way to share the gospel in today’s highly visual culture. The seven interactive sessions are based on stunning films shot all over the globe.
Life Explored will help people uncover what they’re really living for, and see how, in Christ, God meets their deepest desires for happiness.

The links below will be updated from time to time as the classes are carried out. As there are no Follow Up questions, these scribbles are not Q&A oriented.
